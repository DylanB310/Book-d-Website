Book'd

A Controlled Digital Lending Application

Project Summary

Book'd is a web application developed to assist libraries with digital licensing retrictions. It allows staff to better control the digital media they lend out in order to maintain licensing agreements. Patrons can also rent digital media easily and seamlessly.
